LeaderShip

ABS Inc. USA has participated in various large scale quality & compliance system projects for Fortune 500 companies. ABS Inc. USA provided leadership including project managers and subject matter experts in the areas of CAPA management, Investigations management (Deviations, Complaints, Change Controls, Protocols, SOPS, Reports, Site Master Validations Plans), clinical compliance, and quality audits including both clinical data and Regulatory filings. Our team of experts has experience with a variety of audit types including FDA, third party EU /WHO/audits, corporate internal audit programs, as well as Supplier audit and Clinical Trial Site audits. Our ABS experts help clients to achieve full regulatory compliance (Methods, Equipment, Facilities, Utilities, Part 11 CFR). We bring expert consultants who know the regulatory requirements of CFR parts 11, 210, 211 and 820, ISOs.
